How Our AI Works

Four stages of authentication and valuation intelligence

01
Archive Cross-Reference
The AI compares the lot against our archive of 8 million documented garments, including original atelier records, press photographs, and auction histories dating to 1900.
02
Material Analysis
Fabric composition, stitching technique, labelling evolution, and construction method are all matched against the house's documented production practices for the relevant period.
03
Market Modelling
Comparable lot performance across 47 auction houses is processed to generate a confidence-weighted estimate range, adjusted for current market conditions and collector demand.
04
Human Review
Every AI assessment is reviewed by a senior specialist before publication. The machine identifies. The expert certifies. Both signatures appear in the catalogue entry.
