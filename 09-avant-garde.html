What happens when
AI designs without fear?

The conventional fashion designer operates under several constraints: commercial viability, seasonal trend adjacency, material availability, and the risk of the catastrophically unwearable. Our AI has none of these constraints. We gave it access to 140 years of fashion archives, the complete history of art from cave paintings to generative models, and one instruction: design what no human would dare to.

The results were, as expected, mostly unwearable. But in the 8 objects that survived human review — not because they were commercially acceptable, but because they were undeniably, uncomfortably true — something new exists. A fashion object designed without the need to be liked. The avant-garde, finally, without the performance of it.
