The
System
Rules
01 — Objectivity
The AI applies the same systematic rigour to fashion that Swiss designers applied to typography: every decision justified by function, every recommendation anchored in measurable proportion and proven aesthetic law.
02 — The Grid
Fashion, like typography, has an underlying grid. Body proportions. Visual weight. Colour relationships. The AI reads this grid in your body and selects pieces that sit correctly within it.
03 — Clarity
No mood boards. No vague language. The AI speaks in specifics: this silhouette, this fabric weight, this colourway — with measurable reasons for each. Helvetica doesn't apologise for being readable. Neither do we.
04 — Permanence
The Swiss style was designed to be universal and timeless. Our AI selects for the same quality: garments that will not embarrass you in five years. Trends are ephemeral. The grid is forever.
